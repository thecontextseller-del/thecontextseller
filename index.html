The problem I solve

Founders want to automate and scale. Their AI keeps giving them everyone else's answer.

The tools are already there. What's missing is context — how your business runs, what matters to you, how your team makes decisions. Without it, AI produces generic work that needs hours of fixing and never quite fits.

I close that gap in three moves: I build the context your AI needs, I design the agent systems that run on it, and I train the people who own them.

Context
Your business logic, workflows, and judgment — structured into a layer your AI can think with. This is what makes its output yours, not everyone's.
Systems
Agents and multi-agent systems that take real work off your team — research, content, pricing, outreach — and run it in production, not in a demo.
People
Personalised training built around your team's actual work, so the capability stays after I leave and shows up in what they ship.
